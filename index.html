Our Story

Cultivating Tradition & Culinary Heritage

Dera & Diwan brings the heart and soul of traditional Pakistani hospitality to your table. Rooted in the rich cultural legacy of the subcontinent, our name represents two spaces of gatherings: the rustic warmth of the Dera and the courtly sophistication of the Diwan.

Our chefs employ ancestral slow-cooking techniques, marinating premium cuts of meat overnight and cooking them over live charcoal embers or in hand-beaten iron Karahis. Every dish represents a culinary tradition passed down through generations, utilizing hand-ground spices and fresh local ingredients.
